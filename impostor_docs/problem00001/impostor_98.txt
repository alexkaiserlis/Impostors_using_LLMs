His body aches, feeling awful, stuck with the pain. It has been so long since he and Nisi were separated, and if he feels this terrible, then she must be too. The sound of quick, light digging and the thumping of an animal heartbeat catch his attention. As he squints into the darkness, he sees Sofer, a small daemon, freeing him from his restraints. Despite the taboo, he can't help but curl around her and let her warmth ease his pain.
