Fiona and Ayesha unexpectedly come across each other in a hallway, and they quickly recognize each other. Alice, one of their mutual friends, calls Fiona to speak with Ayesha, shocking her. Ayesha overhears their conversation and is disturbed to hear about zombies. Ayesha hastily leaves and walks in on a meeting with Dr. Seyers and others, where she is ignored and belittled. Alice pulls her out to explain everything.
