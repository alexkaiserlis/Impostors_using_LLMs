Your eyes opened, scanning the room with slightly dazed wariness. You weren't home, but in a room with grey walls and a glass front. Several cameras were pointed at you. You felt panic rise within you, but you forced it down for your sake and your child's.

"Ahh, good morning, sunshine!", an unknown voice chirped. You didn't reply, kept on checking the room for any details that could help you make out where you were. You were sat in a stool, hands chained to the arms of it. You couldn't move an inch.

"Ignoring me? Well, that will change soon enough," the woman said again.

"Who are you?", you asked, keeping your voice level.

"Me? Oh, I suppose Sherlock never mentioned me. I'm Eurus. Sherlock's sister. You're Sherlock's and Mycroft's sister...."
