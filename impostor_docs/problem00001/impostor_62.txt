Sherlock returns home after a long absence, where John is filled with overpowering emotions of sorrow, regret, guilt, fear, and love. They sit together without embracing, and despite the pain they're going through, Sherlock finds warmth and solace in John's presence. They make plans for dinner the next day, showing a mix of weariness and love, while Sherlock also deals with the reappearance of his nemesis, Moriarty. John struggles with his feelings towards Sherlock at a dreary, monotonous dinner party, and their evening ends with a bittersweet moment that resonates deeply within them.
