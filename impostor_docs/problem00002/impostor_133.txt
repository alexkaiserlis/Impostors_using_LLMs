Mired down by his explanation, the feeling of Douglas-Hamilton’s gaze upon him made Dominic uncomfortable. Although a guard had remained outside, he wanted the meeting to be over. The figure presented the documents requested by his co-conspirator, evidence that the scheme was in full motion. Laying the documents on the desk, Dominic avoided eye contact. "Our little plan is progressing smoothly," stated the figure. Dominic looked up, his face a mask of disinterest. "It seems that we shall have no issue achieving each of our objectives.” The figure smirked, pleased. Dominic had never felt so alone, knowing he was completely entangled in the conspiracy.
